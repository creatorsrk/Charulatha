About Charulatha

Welcome to the enchanting world of Charulatha, where the ancient art of Veena meets contemporary musical expression. With over two decades of dedicated practice and performance, Charulatha has established herself as one of the most respected Veena artists of her generation.

Trained under the guidance of legendary masters, she brings a unique blend of traditional Carnatic music techniques with innovative interpretations that resonate with both classical purists and modern audiences.

Her performances are not just concerts; they are spiritual journeys that transport listeners to a realm of divine music, where each note tells a story and every raga paints an emotion.
